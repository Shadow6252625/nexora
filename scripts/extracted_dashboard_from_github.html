Frequently Asked Questions

What are the fees for swapping?

Fees range from 0.01% to 1% depending on the liquidity pool configuration. Stable pairs typically have lower fees.

How is the APR calculated?

APR is based on 24h trading fees compounded annually, plus any additional liquidity mining rewards active for that pool.

Is the protocol audited?

Yes, Nexus smart contracts have been audited by OtterSec and Zellic. You can find the reports in our documentation.
